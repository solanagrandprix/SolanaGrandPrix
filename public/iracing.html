About iRacing Integration

Connect your iRacing account to automatically sync your league and hosted practice session data. This integration uses OAuth authentication and never stores your iRacing password.

What gets synced:

  • League practice and race sessions
  • Hosted practice sessions
  • Lap times and best lap records
  • Incident counts (when available)
  • Session attendance and participation

Data Privacy: Your iRacing connection information is private and only used to sync your session data. Your tokens are stored securely and can be revoked at any time by disconnecting your account.
